Threats, Risk & Incident

One should know the threat & risk, to handle the incidents with less damage. Threat & Risk are involve in every phase of life, so as with digital world. So the complete awareness about these terminologies makes the user experience much better.

Threats- Threats are possibilities of damage that can occur

Risks- Risks are defined as the probability of threat encounter with the physical or internal weakness of an individual or organisation.

Now if we look in our daily life there are various type of online threats like phishing, spam, social networking which will discuss in further section. Here the vulnerabilities are the weak passwords, disclosure of information regarding credit/debit cards etc. So when these threats encountered with the above mentioned vulnerabilities risk of an incident occurrence increases.

  3. Spam

  4. Malware
