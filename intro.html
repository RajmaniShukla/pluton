Bharatiya Interface for E-Security & Help Management

BHISHM, Bhartiya Interface for E-Security and Help Management is a platform where user can file and manage all the complaints regarding any incident concerned to digital transaction and fraud. With the Digital India movement, there has been a rapid growth in the number of digital users. Now people prefer to go cashless than ever before. But there is a lack of credibility in Digital Transaction. There may be few incidents, which create doubts in the mind of users regarding Digital Transaction. BHISHM tries to safeguard the belief of user in Digital India.

In case of any incident user can login with his UID and select the victim bank account. Automated complaint will be filled to all the concern department which includes, related branch of the bank, support centre of bank, cyber cell. User can also lodge complaint at his local police station also.

All type of user are welcome, whether individual or businessperson. We are here to serve all. All the complaints and acknowledgements will be drafted in form of report. User can use the reports further to reach Ombudsmen or Court. All the reports are digitally signed from related department.
